Bonelab is a virtual reality action-adventure game developed by Stress Level Zero, serving as a spiritual successor to Boneworks. Released in 2022, it combines a physics-driven sandbox with a mysterious, immersive storyline that begins with the player escaping execution and diving into a surreal underground facility. One of its standout features is the ability to switch between different avatars, each with unique body proportions and stats that affect gameplay. With a focus on player freedom, Bonelab offers a mix of combat, puzzle-solving, and exploration across diverse environments. The game supports extensive modding, allowing players to create and share custom content like maps, weapons, and avatars. Designed for VR platforms such as Meta Quest 2 and PC VR, Bonelab pushes the boundaries of interactive physics and experimental gameplay in virtual reality.
